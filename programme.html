Edexcel IGCSE Programme

Compulsory core subjects are studies alongside a number of options. Students usually take between 7-9 IGCSEs as part of the two years programme or 5 and 7 subjects as part of an Intensive IGCSE course. We recommend students to take 7 subjects and during IGCSEs, students will fine-tune their subject choices to target career ambitions more closely.

  • Suitable for students 13+ years old

  • Pre-IGCSE Stage 1 and Stage 2

  • Normal or Intensive IGCSE Program

  • Average class of 12

  • Set the path for progression to A Levels or Advanced Level Foundation
